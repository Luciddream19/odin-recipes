Classic Savory Deviled Eggs

Description

These classic deviled eggs are delightful. They're stuffed with a creamy blend of mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and vinegar. I like to add garlic powder and garnish them with fresh dill.

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-cooked eggs, halved
  • one quater cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon rice wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon chopped fresh dill (Optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon agrlic powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 12 sprigs fresh dill(Optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Scoop egg yolks into a bowl and set egg whites aside.
  3. Add mayonnaise, vinegar, mustard, 1/2 teaspoon chopped dill, garlic powder, and salt to the egg yolks; mash well until smooth.
  4. Spoon or pipe yolk mixture into egg whites. Garnish with dill sprigs.
  5. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
