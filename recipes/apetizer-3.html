Special Deviled Eggs

Description

Learn how to make deviled eggs with relish and horseradish sauce. These unique ingredients set these deviled eggs apart from the rest!

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
  • 2 tablespoons prepared horseradish
  • 1 tablespoon prepared mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika, or as needed, for ganish
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Place eggs in a medium saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat. Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot water, cool and peel.
  2. Slice each egg in half lengthwise and remove yolks; set aside egg white halves and place yolks in a medium bowl.
  3. Use a fork to mash yolks, then mix in mayonnaise, relish, onion, horseradish, and mustard until well combined.
  4. Use a spoon or pastry bag to fill egg white halves with yolk mixture. Garnish with paprika, salt, and pepper.
  5. Chill covered in the refrigerator until serving.
